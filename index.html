Timber, as simple as its name, is a collection characterised by essential lines, that lend themselves to be interpreted according to the finishes and material combinations chosen.

Precise lines draw a solid and comfortable figure. Balanced proportions outline a collection that shows how simplicity can be expressed through different shapes, coatings and combinations:

Timber lends itself to being interpreted according to wood, leather or fabric finishes and to the combinations of colours and materials to enter public and private spaces that require concrete and lasting solutions.
